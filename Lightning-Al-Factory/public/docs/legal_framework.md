## 3. Risk Mitigation & Fallback Clause (Safe Harbor)
In the event of construction interruption, withdrawal of developer funding, or regulatory limitations preventing the completion of all 7 floors:

- **Primary Safeguard**: Lightning AI Factory (@Wshao777) shall retain **absolute ownership/usage rights of at least one completed floor** (Priority: 4th Floor) as a minimum risk guarantee.
- **Remaining Assets**: Any other completed floors or structural assets shall be allocated proportionally based on initial contributions.
